Community Design | ID CARD

When designing an app, the input of users from the intended community is vital to creating a truly relevant app. In this design we propose ID CARD, an anonymous forum app for university students.

Motivation and Community of Interest

University student communities do not have an anonymous space designed specifically for them online. The ID CARD platform allows students to anonymously ask questions and express their thoughts on various categories:

  • Classes
  • Work
  • Life
  • Clubs/Organizations
  • Hobbies
